# Forge-Learning ⚒️

## About

Forge-Learning is the technical training ground of **Project Odyssey** — a long-term journey to master programming, software engineering, robotics, AI, and advanced engineering systems.

The goal is to develop the ability to design, build, and understand complex systems from the ground up.
